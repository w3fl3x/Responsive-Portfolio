About Me

My name is Huy Nguyen. I graduated from University of Washington with a Bachelor of Chemistry and now I am currently enrolled in UW Coding Bootcamp. What I am most excited about this class is to challenge myself to learn new coding skills and to use what I learn to get a job in the tech industry.

In my spare time I enjoy playing tennis and drawing. I do not get to play tennis much anymore as I injured my wrist and still waiting for it to fully heal. It will be nice to be able to play tennis without pain when my wrist is fully healed.

My other passion is anything car related. I enjoy changing each parts with something that is better to improve on what came from the factory, from the visual of the car to improving on the overall performance. It's a challenge to find the right combination of parts to make the improvement all while still keeping the overall reliability of the car. I often will help friends work on their cars, help them figure out what is wrong and researching a solution.
